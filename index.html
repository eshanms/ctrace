What is a Carbon Footprint?

Your carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by our actions. Globally, the average carbon footprint is close to 4 tons per person annually. To avoid global temperature crises, we need to bring this down under 2 tons by 2030.
